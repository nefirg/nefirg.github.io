Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to make known to everyone that a chamber of Commerce is a voluntary partnership of businesses and professionals that work together to build a healthy economy and improve the quality of life in their communities. The purpose is to inform everyone in the city/community of any developments, such as with the Covid-19 vaccine booster shots, and with any political changes that may occur in town or recent business openings.
This website will show you the importance of being a part of your community and how your influence builds trust and grows your business as well as helps your community know that you exist. You will see how much fun and informative it can be to get to know your fellow community members. You will benefit greatly from the healthy business exposure, recent markets, and job openings in your local area. There will be a local weather information tab to check how the weather tends to influence our community on a regular basis, so if you're weather station is down, tv cuts off, a bad radio, or just lack of television, grab your laptop, grab your car and go to your local wifi hotspot and search us up @ www.rockdale-commerce.us.

Audience

The target customers is anyone that is a part of the community. Local Businesses are always welcome to join and see what events we have planned. You can also promote your business by adding it on the site for a small percentage of admissions for all those who attend the event.
If you like to have fun with family, friends, and socialize then feel free to join us at our events, some are pay to have fun and some are completely and absolutely FREE! There are many ways to get a hold of your local business and if you want to network with those businesses around you then going to these events will truly be to your benefit!

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
